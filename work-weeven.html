We Even

Contemporary Dance Duet

A duet exploring the urgency of real human connection. A physical conversation about coexistence. Premiered December 2018, New York.

Watch video ↗

We Even is a duet between a woman and a man that explores the urgency of real human connection. Instead of seeking answers in books or theories, the piece invites us to experience what it means to truly live together through presence, movement, and shared vulnerability.

The performance is driven by a physical dialogue where bodies speak before words, moving through tension, harmony, and silence. The two dancers embody moments of confrontation and intimacy, revealing the complexity and beauty of coexistence.

Accompanied by an original soundscape, the piece invites the audience to witness a relationship built not on ideas, but on lived emotion and mutual recognition.

Created following a residency in New York (November–December 2018). World premiere: New York, December 1, 2018.
