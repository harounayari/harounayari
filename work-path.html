2019

Path

Contemporary Dance Solo

The journey everyone takes from a starting point toward another state of life — through internal conflict, descent into the self, and unexpected emotional responses.

Watch video ↗ Press coverage ↗

Path captures the journey that everyone takes from a starting point toward another state of life. Along the way, it passes through moments of internal conflict, a battle within oneself against all established order.

The descent into the self marks the beginning of all disorder — a deep internal struggle that profoundly affects both body and mind, physically and emotionally, leaving behind unexpected emotional, cognitive, and psychological responses.

Through the dialogue of three bodies in space, the work questions consciousness, identity, and perception. Inspired by philosophical and psychological theories, the choreography reveals involuntary gestures, emotional impulses, and invisible sensations that shape our existence. A journey through doubt, confrontation, and the search for balance — where the body becomes both the question and the answer.
