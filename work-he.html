2017

HE

Contemporary Dance-Theatre

Three dancers — a triangle of bodies exploring the fragile line between inner world and external reality. The self, the superego, and the other.

Watch video ↗

HE explores the tension between the inner and outer worlds of the human being. Through the dialogue of three bodies in space, the work questions consciousness, identity, and perception.

Three dancers share a space of tension and reflection, exploring the fragile line between the inner world and external reality. Through complex, involuntary movements, the piece questions identity, perception, and the unconscious.

A triangle of bodies, each unique yet connected by breath and impulse, reveals a living theorem: the self, the superego, and the other. The stage becomes a space of unanswered questions, where dance is both a search and a revelation.
